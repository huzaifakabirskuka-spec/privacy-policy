Overview

Koyi Komfuta (“the App”) is an educational mobile application designed to teach users how to operate and repair computers in the Hausa language. We value your privacy and strive to be transparent about how data is handled.

1. Information we do not collect

By default, the App does not collect any personally identifiable information. Specifically, we do not collect:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, or postal address (unless you voluntarily provide them via contact points outside the app).
  • Location data or precise geolocation.
  • Biometric data (fingerprints, facial recognition, etc.).
  • Social security numbers or other national IDs.

2. Information we may collect

While the App is designed to work offline, it may use the following local or non-personal data:

  • Local progress data: Lesson completion and small app settings are stored on the device (for example, which lessons you marked complete). This stays on the device unless you export or back it up yourself.
  • Crash reports & diagnostics (optional): If enabled or available through the platform, anonymous crash logs or diagnostics may be collected to help us fix bugs. These logs do not include personal identifiers.

3. How we use information

Any non-personal data collected is used solely to:

  • Improve app stability and performance.
  • Fix bugs that affect functionality.
  • Improve the user experience and lesson quality.

4. Third-party services

The App may rely on third-party services for distribution, hosting, or optional analytics. These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Play Services Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Privacy Statement

We do not sell or share personal data with advertising networks.

5. Children’s privacy

The App is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app, contact us at the email below and we will take steps to remove that data.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the “Last updated” date at the top will be updated. We recommend checking this page periodically.
